Notable findings

The things most likely causing the "confusion" — worth a look before relying on the menus.

  1. Two Services pages have no way in

    /services/port-network and /services/port-services aren't in the navbar mega-menu, the footer, or linked from any other page. They only render if someone types the URL. Either add them to navigation or fold them into the Solutions/Industries menus.

  2. The mega-menu's "SERVICES" column doesn't point at /services

    Its three links (Custom Processing, Distribution Support, Technical Assistance) actually go to /custom-steel-processing, /supply-chain, and /technical-steel-selection — duplicates of the Solutions column, not the real Services pages above.

  3. The Admin panel is completely unlinked from the public site

    All 17 /admin/* pages only become reachable once someone knows to visit /admin/login directly — there's no footer or hidden link into it, which is expected for an admin area but worth confirming is intentional.

  4. A breadcrumb on Port Services links to itself

    ServiceHero.tsx's "Our Services" breadcrumb on /services/port-services points back at /services/port-services instead of a Services index (which doesn't exist).

  5. Footer's "Return Policy" is a dead link

    In the Policy column, href="#" goes nowhere — there's no return policy page to point it at yet.

  6. Industries has no footer presence

    All 6 industry pages are reachable only through the navbar's mega-menu — if a visitor lands on a page without scrolling up (e.g. from a search result) and the mega-menu is missed, there's no footer path to them.
